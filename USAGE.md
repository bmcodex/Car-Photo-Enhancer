# Usage Guide

## Getting Started

### 1. Sign In

Visit the application and click "Sign In to Start" to authenticate using Manus OAuth.

### 2. Upload a Photo

- Click "Select Photo" or drag and drop an image
- Supported formats: JPEG, PNG, WebP
- Maximum file size: 50MB

## Editing Your Photos

### Using Presets

1. **Gloss** - Perfect for clean, polished car photos
   - Increases brightness and contrast
   - Enhances highlights
   - Best for: Daytime photos, showroom photos

2. **Metallic Reflection** - For metallic paint finishes
   - Boosts reflections and shine
   - Increases saturation slightly
   - Best for: Metallic or pearl paint

3. **Deep Paint** - For rich, saturated colors
   - Increases color saturation
   - Enhances vibrancy
   - Best for: Colorful cars, artistic shots

4. **Night Neon** - For dramatic lighting effects
   - Reduces brightness
   - Increases contrast and saturation
   - Best for: Night photography, neon signs

5. **Wheel Detail Enhancer** - For sharp wheel details
   - Increases sharpness and contrast
   - Perfect for: Close-up wheel photos

### Manual Adjustments

#### Editor Tab
Adjust individual filters:
- **Brightness**: -50 to +50 (darker to brighter)
- **Contrast**: -50 to +50 (flat to dramatic)
- **Saturation**: -50 to +100 (grayscale to vivid)
- **Vibrance**: -50 to +100 (muted to vibrant)
- **Sharpen**: 0 to +50 (soft to crisp)

#### License Plate Tab
1. Set blur radius (5-50px)
2. Toggle area preview to see blur region
3. Click "Apply Blur" to protect privacy

#### Reflection Removal Tab
1. Adjust intensity (0-100%)
2. Click "Remove Reflections"
3. Reduces glare and unwanted reflections

#### Color Correction Tab
1. Adjust brightness for lighting
2. Adjust contrast for definition
3. Adjust saturation for color intensity
4. Adjust temperature for white balance
5. Click "Apply Correction"

#### Comparison Tab
- Drag the slider to compare before and after
- Touch-friendly on mobile devices
- Helps verify your edits

## Advanced Features

### Edit History

- Track all changes applied to your photo
- Click "Undo" to revert the last change
- Edit history is shown in the info card

### Before/After Comparison

- Use the interactive slider to compare versions
- Drag left/right to reveal different areas
- Perfect for verifying adjustments

### Download Your Photo

1. Click the "Download" button
2. Photo is saved as JPEG with high quality
3. Filename includes timestamp

## Troubleshooting

### Photo Won't Upload

- Check file size (max 50MB)
- Verify file format (JPEG, PNG, WebP)
- Try a different browser
- Clear browser cache

### Edits Look Wrong

- Click "Reset" to start over
- Use "Undo" to revert last change
- Compare with original using slider
- Try different preset

### Download Issues

- Check browser download settings
- Ensure sufficient disk space
- Try a different browser
- Check file permissions

### Performance Issues

- Close other browser tabs
- Clear browser cache
- Use a modern browser
- Try a smaller image
